The  Professional Mentorship Program is now live! The program enables a one-on-one professional learning relationship between a mentor and mentee for members to expand their knowledge, leadership skills and enhance professional competencies. 

This year's pilot program has received high applicant interest with 30 compatible mentors and mentees to participate. The pilot program will run from April to July 2017. If you are interested to participate in the next phase, please complete the interest form available for members to be considered and matched.
