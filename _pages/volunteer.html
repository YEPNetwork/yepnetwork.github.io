Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission is to inspire, educate and empower the Ethiopian professional community to make a positive impact in the world and Our Vision is to create a strong community that shares ideas, skills and resources to enrich lives.

Donate with Trust

We are committed to managing all contributions to YEP with the highest level of financial integrity and keeping you informed about how your resources are used.

Giving Options

Nearly 90% of our funding comes from committed individuals like you who choose to use their financial resources as an expression of their highest values and hopes for the world.

In addition to making a one-time donation, we invite you to consider becoming a Global Citizen Monthly Donor by committing to a monthly contribution.

Or, you can join one of our special Major Giving Programs to provide long-term support. A Vision Keeper commits to an annual gift of $1,000 to $4,999 per year for five years, while a Stewardship Circle member invests $5,000 or more per year for five years.

We also have Corporate Giving options available.
