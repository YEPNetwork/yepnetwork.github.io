Mission & Vision

"No one person can make it in life without the support and guidance of other people in their lives." - Shimelse Mekonnen

Our Mission

To inspire, educate and empower the Ethiopian professional community to make a positive impact in the world.

Our Vision

A strong community that shares ideas, skills and resources to enrich lives.

Goals

  • Creating networking opportunties for members to share resources
  • Providing a platform that enables members to share job opportunities
  • Providing resources to complement specific skill development such as leadership, public speaking, entrepreneurship, etc.
