Mission & Vision

"When spider webs unite, they can tie up a lion" - Ethiopian Proverb

Our Mission

To inspire, educate and empower the Ethiopian professional community to make a positive impact in the world.

Our Vision

The vision that informs YEP is a build a strong community that shares ideas, skills and resources to enrich lives.
