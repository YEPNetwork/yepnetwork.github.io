Mission & Vision

"When spider webs unite, they can tie up a lion" - Ethiopian Proverb

Our Mission

To inspire, educate and empower the Ethiopian professional community to make a positive impact in the world.

Our Vision

YEP is driven by the vision to build a strong community that shares ideas, skills, and resources to enrich lives.

We also envision a grand future, complete with a YEP headquarters that can serve as a one-stop resource center for members and the community at large.
