Programs

YEP was created in 2009 to fill the need for a broader platform for the Ethiopian diaspora across all professions to connect, share experiences and valuable resources and pursue community development initiatives. Since YEP came into existence, it has taken a number of steps to fill the gap that existed between aspiring mentors and young professionals, among professionals in different fields, and between professionals and new comers, the business society, and the larger Ethiopian community. YEP's programs are an integral part of this vision.
